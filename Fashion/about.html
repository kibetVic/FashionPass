About Fashion Pass

Our Mission

At Fashion Pass ,our mission is to offer accessible, top-quality fashion and design education that empowers everyone to build the skills and confidence needed to shine in the fashion world.

Our Vision

Fashion Pass is dedicated to providing top-quality fashion and design education, integrating modern technology and hands-on training to empower students of all ages and backgrounds to achieve excellence in the fashion industry.

Our History

Founded in 2014, Fashion Pass began as a small tailoring center with a single sewing machine and one designer, created to bridge the fashion skills gap in our community. Over the years, we have grown into a full fashion and design school equipped with modern machines and spacious classrooms. In 2025, the school was accredited by NITA to offer courses in tailoring, fashion design, embroidery, and crocheting, as well as Grade I, II, and III exams and certification.
